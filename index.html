About The Game

A brutally realistic story-driven startup simulation where 99% fail and only 1% become unicorns. Make critical decisions, manage resources, navigate crises, and see if you have what it takes to build the next billion-dollar company.

How to Play

  • Make decisions by clicking choice buttons (or press 1, 2, 3)
  • Press Enter to continue through events
  • Watch your resources carefully - money runs out fast!
  • Reach 1000+ users, $10K+ MRR, and 80+ product quality to attempt exit
  • Need $50M+ valuation for a successful unicorn exit
